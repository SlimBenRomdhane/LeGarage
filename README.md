# LeGarage Management Application

LeGarage Application is designed to streamline operations in automotive and mechanical workshops.  
It manages **work orders, tasks, clients, vehicles, payments, spare parts, stock replenishment, and staff assignments** with audit-safe workflows and technician-first ergonomics.

---

## 🚀 What the App Does

- **Work Orders & Tasks**: Create, assign, and track tasks with labor, parts, and service costs.
- **Clients & Vehicles**: Register clients and their vehicles, linking them to work orders for full traceability.
- **Payments & Invoicing**: Handle multiple payment types (advance, installment, etc.) with audit-safe records.
- **Spare Parts & Inventory**: Categorize parts, track stock levels, and manage replenishment from providers.
- **Workshops & Staff**: Define workshops, assign technicians, and manage availability and roles.
- **Audit & Metadata**: Built-in audit fields ensure lifecycle transparency and compliance.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Backend**: ASP.NET Core (REST APIs, EF Core for ORM and migrations)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **Frontend (Web)**: Blazor (Server/WebAssembly)
- **Frontend (Mobile)**: .NET MAUI (cross-platform iOS, Android, Windows, macOS)
- **UI Frameworks**: Bootstrap for technician-first ergonomics
- **Deployment**: Azure (managed identity, secure connectivity)
